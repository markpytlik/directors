№ 01

Hype Williams

United States
Portrait pending

No single director shaped the look of late-90s and 2000s hip-hop more decisively. Fish-eye lenses, Vaseline glow, color-blocked sets, high-fashion absurdity — Hype Williams turned the rap video into a pop-art object.
